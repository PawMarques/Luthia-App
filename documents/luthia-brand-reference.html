Character
The mark is a cross fleury (cross botonnée) — a heraldic cross where each arm terminates in a trefoil bud.
Used historically by craft guilds and instrument makers. Bold, symmetrical, and immediately recognisable at any scale.
Avoid
Rotating the mark. Stretching or distorting. Placing on low-contrast backgrounds without adjustment.
