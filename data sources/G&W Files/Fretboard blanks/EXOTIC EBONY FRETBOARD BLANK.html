Exotic Ebony Fretboard Blanks for guitar or bass builds.

Already planed.

To save you some time, we offer multiple choices of customization you can choose from, such as calibration, pre-radiusing and compound radiusing.
Radiusing operations are CNC made and don't provide a ready-to-use product.

The pictures presented here work as an example, as wood is never the same and can't be replicated.


DIMENSIONS:
Guitar: 500-530 x 70-75 x 6-8mm
Bass 70mm wide: 700-730 x 70 x 6-8mm
Bass 75mm wide: 700-730 x 75 x 6-8mm
Bass 80mm wide: 700-730 x 80 x 6-8mm
Bass 100mm wide: 700-730 x 100 x 6-8mm

LABELS:
B-Stock: Below B grade - can have signs of worms, cracks, holes, dents and noticeable grain patterns. Some still can be used as guitar or mandolin fretboards, other must be cut in small pieces to do bridges or other products not necessarily guitar-related.
Returns are not accepted for these blanks.

DISCLAIMER:
Bass fretboards cannot be shipped through the CTT shipping option due to their length (over 60cm).
Please keep this in mind when adding to cart and during checkout, otherwise you'll be notified in order to change the shipping option to either UPS options - Standard or Express.
