Beautiful White Limba neck blanks for guitar and bass builds.
Disclaimer:
White Limba is a wood that is particularly subjected to insect attacks on the living tree, therefore you might find tiny holes in the blanks you purchased.
For this reason we do not take responsibility over this matter, neither we accept refunds or trades.
These high-quality blanks are planed to thickness (please allow for a few millimeters of variance) but are not fully calibrated (Calibration/Thickness Sanding service is also available). 
Available Dimensions:
  • Guitar Neck Blank: 700mm x 95-100mm x 20mm
  • Bass Neck Blank: 880-900mm x 100-110mm x 20mm
  • One-Piece Guitar Neck: 750mm x 70mm x 70mm
  • One-Piece Bass Neck: 930-940mm x 100-110mm x 30mm
  • Two-Neck Blank: 750mm x 105mm x 70mm
Customization Options:
  • Scarf Joint Service – Available as an option. Details can be found in the product image labeled “Scarf Joint Service.”
  • Custom Orders – Contact us for specific requirements or unique requests.
Wood Variations & Grading:
Each blank is unique - wood grain and color may vary slightly from the example photos.
  • AA Grade – Our top-tier, premium tonewoods (available exclusively in the Selected Tonewoods section).
  • A Grade – Our standard high-quality blanks free of knots, holes, or cracks.
  • B-Stock – May contain knots or cracks, ideal for laminated builds, solid color finishes, or epoxy filling. B-Stock blanks are non-returnable.
In the Blank Type selection, standard A Grade blanks are labeled simply as "Guitar Neck" or "Bass Neck".
Important Shipping Information:
Due to its length (over 60cm), this item cannot be shipped via CTT. Please choose UPS Standard or UPS Express at checkout. If CTT is selected, we will notify you to adjust your shipping method.
