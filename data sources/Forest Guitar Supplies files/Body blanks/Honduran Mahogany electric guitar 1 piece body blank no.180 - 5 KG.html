Authenticity: You will receive the exact piece shown in the photos
Reference: Second image shows a real Stratocaster body outline
Origin: Made in Spain with high standards of quality and sustainability

Why this wood?

Honduran Mahogany is one of the most prized tonewoods in guitar building, used in iconic models like Les Paul and PRS.
It offers a strong midrange, warm and organic tone, great stability, and outstanding workability. It cuts, sands, and routes effortlessly and accepts stains and finishes beautifully.

Limited stock — once these high-quality blanks are gone, they won’t be restocked at this price.

CITES Information

Honduran Mahogany is CITES-listed.
No permit required within the EU.
For buyers outside the EU, please contact us before purchasing.

A note on body weight

We select each blank for stability, resonance, and tonal character. Weight is a factor, but not the main one.
Lighter woods can sound open and responsive; denser ones provide depth, definition, and sustain. Each blank has its own voice.

Our goal is not to offer “ultra-light” pieces, but well-balanced tonewood with reliable acoustic behavior — ensuring instruments with character, precision, and long-lasting quality.
